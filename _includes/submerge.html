Submerge

an event for high school students to submerge into…

college:

ST. LOUIS CHRISTIAN COLLEGE
During SUBMERGE, students will get a deeper look at college life in a Christian setting by experiencing a weekend on our campus in St. Louis, Missouri.

city:

LOVEtheLOU
LOVEtheLOU is an organization designed to bring beauty back to urban neighborhoods in St. Louis. Your High Schoolers will be submerged in an experience that will open their eyes to the beauty of St. Louis.

christ:

KIAIRA HOWARD // JEREMIAH HOWZE
We will be submerged in Christ through worship by Kiaira Howard as well as deep, practical teaching by Jeremiah Howze; both SLCC alumni.
