Details

Here's some logistic information to help you plan your weekend. More details to come.

Schedule
We'll kick off the weekend with a main session at 7:00 pm Friday and conclude by 5:00 pm on Saturday. Registration will begin at 4:00 pm on Friday.
Housing
We will have a limited amount of on-campus housing, available on a first-come basis. Groups being housed on campus will need to bring bedding and toiletries.
We will also have a limited amount of space at area churches for groups to stay overnight, also available on a first-come basis. Each church will have different accommodations, but groups should not anticipate mattresses/couches/etc. being provided by the church.
Hotels
Quality Inn, Dunn Road (at the intersection of Graham, right next to a Starbucks & Arby's), Florissant, MO
Comfort Inn & Suites, Douglass Palmer Place (at the intersection of Lindbergh/US-67 & I-270), Hazelwood, MO
LaQuinta Inn, Taylor Road (at the intersection of Linbergh/US-67 & I-270), Hazelwood, MO
Meals
We will be providing a late-night snack on Friday, but please feed your group dinner before arrival.
Breakfast and lunch will be provided on Saturday.
Transportation
Transportation will be required for our LOVEtheLOU service project on Saturday. Please plan on transporting your group to and from the project site.
