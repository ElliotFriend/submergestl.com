About

At Submerge, you will dive deeper into:

  • College

    Deciding where to attend college is one of the most important decisions you're going to make.

    We would love for every student to consider attending a Bible college; for valuable preparation to further God's Kingdom in a ministry career, or to gain a foundation for future studies.

    Come submerge yourself in SLCC's campus to get a taste of what Bible college is all about.

  • City

    We LOVE our city! And we think you will too.

    We will spend a portion of Saturday working on a project in the city with LOVEtheLOU, and learn how they fulfill their mission:

    "Where there is nothing we will create.
    Where there is brokenness we will heal.
    Where there is good we will join."

    Come submerge yourself in the city of St. Louis as we partner with local organization, LOVEtheLOU, founded by 2003 graduate, Lucas Rouggly.

  • Christ

    At the center of everything is Christ.

    During a time of year when the passion and excitement that was cultivated over summer can easily be overshadowed by the struggles of day-to-day life, we want to help bring your focus back onto Christ.

    Come submerge yourself in Christ and be taught, challenged, and used to further His Kindom.
